# Fee Structure

Source: Google Sheets (shared spreadsheet)
Retrieved: 2026-02-03

## Fee Schedule

| Item | Fee | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Standard Portal Fee | $2,250 per month (waived) | Waived permanently |
| Premium Equity Tax & Performance | $500 per year | Deducted from client cash facility; includes tax optimisation & performance reporting |
| Premium Option Tax & Performance | $700 per year per client entity | Additional $200 from equity only |
| Adviser Revenue Share | $100 per premium client (Year 1) | Paid to the Adviser for each premium client in year one |
| Morrison Securities Broking Services | As agreed | Additional admin & service fees may apply; Advisers may pass costs on |
| MDA Operator Fee | 22 bps (minimum $594/year) | Payable by Adviser |

## Billing Methods

| Fee Type | Billing Method |
|----------|----------------|
| Premium fees | Deducted from client cash facility |
| ASX fees | Deducted from client cash facility |
| Portfolio-based fees | Calculated daily, charged monthly |
| Portal fee | Billed monthly |

## Key Observations

1. **Premium Tiers:**
   - Equities only: $500/year
   - Equities + Options: $700/year (+$200 for options)

2. **Daily Calculation:**
   - Portfolio-based fees are calculated daily
   - Aligns with code: `annual_amount / 365`

3. **Deduction Source:**
   - Premium fees come from client cash facility
   - Implies need to check cash balance before charging

4. **Adviser Incentive:**
   - $100 per premium client in Year 1
   - Creates revenue share record separate from client fees
