## Key Differences from Standard

| Aspect | Standard (Emerald) | Finexia |
|--------|-------------------|---------|
| Premium Fee | $500/year (Equities), $700/year (Equities+Options) | $500/year (Equities), $700/year (Equities+Options) |
| Portal Fee Waiver | Waived permanently | Waived 12 months, then waived if 50+ paid customers |
| Market Data | Not listed separately | $300/month (paid by adviser) |
| Payment Source | Client cash facility | Client pays directly |

## Notes

1. **Different Payment Model:** Finexia clients pay directly (not deducted from cash facility)
2. **Conditional Waiver:** Standard portal fee waived conditionally (12 months or 50+ customers)
3. **Market Data Fee:** Additional $300/month adviser fee for real-time ASX data
4. **Same Premium Pricing:** $500 equity / $700 equity+options structure matches standard
